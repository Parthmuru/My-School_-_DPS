Dehli Public School

Delhi Public School Society, New Delhi established in 1949 is an internationally renowned educational society. Under the sponsorship of NTPC and strong administration of DPS Society , DPS Vindhyanagar has been recognized as one of the best schools at National Level. The DPS Society (DPSS) is a non-profit, non-proprietary educational body widely recognized for its selfless and unstinting committed to excellence in education.
